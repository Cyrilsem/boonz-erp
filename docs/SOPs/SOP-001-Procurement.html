Phase 3 — Warehouse Receipt
6
Warehouse
📦 Open PO to receive
Go to Pending orders → tap PO → tap Receive. Driver's field report shown per product — qty hints pre-filled.
Field App
↓
7
Warehouse
🏷️ Fill in quantities per product
For each line: received qty, expiry date (multiple batches OK), warehouse location, price per unit.
↓
📋 For items the driver couldn't buy
✗ Mark as "Not purchased"
  • Tap Mark not purchased on the line
  • Card turns red — no stock added
  • Line closes with outcome = not_purchased
  • Never shows as "pending" again
  • Auto-toggled if driver said "not available"
⚠️ Partial delivery
  • Enter actual qty received
  • Driver hint shows expected qty
  • Closes with received_qty < ordered_qty
  • Shortfall visible in order history
↓
8
Warehouse

Tap Confirm receiving — activates only when at least one line is actionable. All field additions also inventoried in the same action.
